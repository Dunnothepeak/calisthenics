Pro Tips
📈 Progressive OverloadWhen basic exercises feel easy, add reps or move to harder progressions — not more exercises.
😴 Rest = GrowthYour muscles grow during recovery. Never skip rest days. Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep.
🥗 Fuel RightEat enough protein (0.7–1g per lb bodyweight). You can't out-train a bad diet.
🔄 Form FirstMaster the basic movement before advancing. Poor form = injury. Watch your reps in slow motion.
💧 HydrationDrink at least 2–3L of water daily. Dehydration kills performance and recovery.
📊 Track EverythingLog your reps and sets each session. Progress you don't track doesn't count.
🧘 Warm Up5–10 min of dynamic warm-up before every session. Saves you from weeks of injury.
⏱️ Rest Between Sets60–90 sec for strength, 30–60 sec for endurance. Don't rush, don't slack.
